## Why the fault pause

`FAULT_POINT:<name>` is printed, the harness reacts in milliseconds â€” but a microtask can commit first. `WELTARI_FAULT_PAUSE_MS` holds the engine at `between_calls`/`pre_commit` so the SIGKILL reliably lands inside the window; `mid_stream` needs no hold (many event-loop yields during streaming, and nothing durable exists in that phase either way).
